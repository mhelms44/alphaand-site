Bio

Alpha Andromeda is a four-piece progressive metal band from [CITY], North Carolina, pairing down-tuned riffs and technical grooves with electronic textures, abrupt transitions, and an offbeat sci-fi streak. The result is heavy, tightly arranged, and deliberately strange without taking itself too seriously.

Formed in 2019, the band has been playing together in one configuration or another since 2011.That history carries into a tightly arranged live set built around heavy riffs, fast transitions, electronic production, and very little standing around.

The band has released music independently since its 2019 debut, Gaze Into The Void, most recently Bird Dream Watcher, Primordial Purple, and Do Not Reply To The Beacon in 2025.
